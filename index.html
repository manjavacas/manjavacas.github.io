The Sustainable Artificial Intelligence Laboratory (SAIL) is part of the Department of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence at the Universidad de Granada. It was created in 2019 to promote AI applications in the energy sector, particularly in the simulation and control of buildings and smart grids. Subsequently, we started working on fighting disinformation through artificial intelligence tools.
